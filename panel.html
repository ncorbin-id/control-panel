Your instructions

Your task is to fire phasers aboard a starship. First, you'll practice the procedure using the excerpt from the Control Panel Instruction Manual. When you're ready, you'll test your ability to fire phasers without guidance from the manual.

  1. Read through the Control Panel Instruction Manual and familiarize yourself with the control panel.
  2. Practice firing phasers a few times on your own.

    When the PF indicator illuminates, select Success to mark the procedure successful and reset the control panel for another firing. If you get stuck, select Reset to reset the control panel back to its original state.

  3. When you're ready, select Test me to test your ability to fire phasers on your own without guidance.
Control Panel Instruction Manual

Phasers

This section of the control panel is used to bring the phaser system online, manage power during operation, and fire phasers. Note that this system can sometimes malfunction.

Fire phasers

  1. On the control panel, flip the SP Switch.

    2. The SP Indicator illuminates. After three seconds, the EB and MA Indicators illuminate.

  2. Set the ES Selector to MA.
  3. Select the FM Button.

    4. The PF Indicator illuminates, indicating successful firing.
